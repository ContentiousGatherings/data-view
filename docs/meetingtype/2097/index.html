Reason: The term 'anarkistiskt upprop' translates to 'anarchist call' or 'anarchist manifesto' in English. While it can imply a specific type of gathering or call to action, it does not directly correspond to one of the predefined valid meeting types listed in your criteria (e.g., *folkmöte*, *strike*, *demonstration*, etc.). The term is more of a *call to action* or *manifest* rather than a formal meeting type.
